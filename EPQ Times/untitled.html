Features

Log, View, Record

Our platform provides an employee view, which allow users the ability to their log in time as well as their log out time. Users are also able to request time off on our platform. Time off requests availability are only viewable up to the number of days in advanced specified by administrators.

Empowering Employees

Many businesses are using outdated and un-efficient ways to keep track of their employee’s time on the job. Whether that be to keep track when an employee clocks in or out or who is on vacation when. We wanted to change that. Being able to effortlessly catalog, store, view, and monitor employee hours makes a world of difference to not only the business but more importantly, its employees.

For Employees...

A universal method to clock in and out, record and monitor time off requests, review impact for employee's time away, as well as view important dates and announcements. You only need to learn one system instead of many! Employees will only need one account. This means instead of a user needing to remember multiple usernames and passwords, they only need to remember one username and one password! This is especially useful to workers who are employed by more than one employer on the platform.

For Employers...

Employers now have the ability to efficiently monitor their employee’s time and requests. Utilizing this universal application will free up business resources, as businesses are not developing and maintaining their application, system, or method. It is all available to you through our reasonably priced service. Click the button below to enter and get pricing options for your company. 

Adminstrators

As our customers never have to worry about maintenance, we have trained technicians and motivators who maintain, service, upadate, and troubleshoot the platform. Administrators click the button below to login. 

Stay Up to Date

Employees are able to view important dates, work shifts schedule, vacation conflict with other employees, and reminders set by their employer. The calendar includes the ability to change between different viewing modes including by day, by week, by month, by year, by agenda view, and a weekly hour countdown tracker.
